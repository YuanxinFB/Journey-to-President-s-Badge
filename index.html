🫱 Right Arm — Proficiency Badges
  • Badges arranged alphabetically (Target Badge always first)
  • 5 badges per row, always centred
  • = Advanced badge (red cloth on uniform)
  • Badges do not overlap the chevrons below
  • Select B for Basic, A for Advanced (A auto-selects B)

  • Row 3: Gold · Silver · Bronze Scholastic Awards
  • Row 4: Long Year · NCO Proficiency · Three Year Service
  • Row 5: Junior Section + One Year Service badges

President's Award Syllabus

The applicant must be 15 years and above and be recommended by his/her Company Captain and Chaplain.

  • Must be an NCO in the Company
  • NCO's Proficiency Star (Advanced Level)
  • Christian Education (Advanced Level)
  • Drill (Advanced Level)
  • Recruitment (Basic Level)
  • At least three years of service in the Senior Section
  • At least six (6) awards from groups A, B, C & D with at least one from each group — minimum 2 Basic + 4 Advanced
